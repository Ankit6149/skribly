# Release legal and compliance checklist

Before a paid public release:

- [ ] trademark/name clearance for Skribly
- [ ] domain and social handle review
- [ ] privacy policy
- [ ] terms of sale/licence
- [ ] refund policy
- [ ] end-user licence terms
- [ ] third-party dependency notices
- [ ] Windows signing certificate or Store signing route
- [ ] Apple Developer ID and notarization
- [ ] CA review of domestic and international sales flow
- [ ] GST registration decision recorded
- [ ] invoice format approved
- [ ] payment-provider KYC completed
- [ ] vulnerability reporting contact
- [ ] data export and deletion behavior documented

This checklist does not replace professional review.
