# Naming and product language

## Brand

**Skribly**

Pronunciation: *scrib-lee*.

## Product noun

A contextual annotation is a **Skrib**.

Examples:

- Add a Skrib
- 3 Skribs on this page
- Show my Skribs
- This Skrib returns with the app

## Positioning

Primary line: **Leave thoughts exactly where they belong.**

Explanatory line: **Add notes, handwriting, arrows, highlights, and reminders directly to any app or webpage. They return when you do.**

## Avoid

- describing Skribly as merely a sticky-notes app
- leading with AI
- calling it a whiteboard
- promises of perfect anchoring in every third-party application
- “Post-it” in product naming or marketing because it is a protected brand
